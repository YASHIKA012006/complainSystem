About

Welcome to the Student Complaint Registration System made by QUARTILE CODER. This platform is designed to provide students with a seamless way to submit complaints regarding academics, facilities, hostel, transport, sports, or any other college-related issues.

Our mission is to ensure transparency, accountability, and faster resolution of student grievances. Students can track the status of their complaints in real-time, receive updates, and communicate directly with the administration for better outcomes.

QUARTILE CODER is committed to creating a supportive and efficient learning environment. This system empowers students to voice their concerns while enabling the administration to address issues systematically, making the college experience smoother and more productive.

Our Mission

To provide a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform for grievance redressal, ensuring every student's voice is heard and addressed promptly.

  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Fast Resolution with Real-time Tracking
  • Secure and Confidential Communication
  • Modern Admin Analytics Dashboard
